About Us

Crafting Dream Kitchens Since Day One

Italian Kitchens Limited is Nairobi's premier destination for premium kitchen solutions. Located on the 2nd floor of the prestigious Western Heights building on Karuna Road, we serve homeowners, designers, and businesses looking to create modern, functional kitchens and living spaces.

Our passion lies in bringing the timeless elegance of Italian design to Kenyan homes. With a curated selection of high-quality kitchen products, expert consultation services, and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we've earned a stellar 4.4-star rating from over 25 satisfied customers.
